NFT Project
It was fortunate that I pursued a major in software programming during my university years, which allowed me to work as a freelancer over the past few years. Working from home enabled me to better take care of my daughters. Unfortunately, we did not consider obtaining any medical insurance when my two daughters were born. Therefore, we have been responsible for paying all the medical bills ourselves. Unexpectedly, Yolanda suffered a fracture last November, and I had to borrow around $10K from relatives for her surgery. The total amount borrowed from my relatives has now exceeded $30K.
As a believer in web 3 and blockchain technology, I conceived the idea of the MAS awareness project. This project aims to raise public awareness about this rare disease while also generating funds. The NFT image is designed to be a half-length portrait of a little girl wearing clothes with patterns on one side, symbolizing cafe-au-lait spots. The portrait will be accompanied by the words 'I Support MAS Patients' on the bottom. Yolanda and I co-designed and hand-drew all the parts of the NFT.
The MAS awareness project consists of a total of 10,000 NFTs, with each NFT priced at 0.09 BNB. The total sales (900 BNB) will be divided into three parts: one-third will be allocated as rewards to referrals, one-third will be donated to an MAS charity organization, and one-third will be used to pay off my family's debts and provide support for future medical and living expenses. I aim for this project to extend beyond traditional charity efforts, so I have additional web3 projects planned. Holders of MAS Awareness NFTs will be eligible for airdrops for the future projects.
